About This Site

When I found that I needed to make a site about llamas, I think to myself, wow, that's never happened before. I figured that between doing class projects and attempting to have a social life, I have approximately 10 hours to build this site. Here's the breakdown:

  • Hour 1: Design Phase.
    I was thinking to challenge myself not to use any framework for this site. But having just built a web app without any framework at all a couple weeks prior, I decided that it was enough to showcase my understanding of HTML, CSS, Javascript. I decided that I will use JQuery to simply the process of manipulating DOM.
  • Hour 2-5: Writing llama-articles.html
    This is the page containing summary, history, behavior, and nutrition of llamas. I decided to include a slider on the history section to show my understanding on manipulating DOM.
  • Hour 6: Writing about.html
  • Hour 7-8:Writing quiz.html
    This is the "meet your llama" section. Similarly, this was also written to show my understanding of DOM and also to showcase a little bit of personality.
  • Hour 9 - 9.5: re-styling, making sure everything works on web and mobile the way I want them to.
    I forgot that making things look pretty take slightly longer than I expect
  • Hour 10: Writing this very page you're currently reading!
    I can't lie. Making a web about llamas is more interesting than I would've thought.
